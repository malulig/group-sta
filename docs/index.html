Group STA

Group static apnea timer for freediving training

About the app

Group STA is a free and ad-free mobile application designed for group static apnea training in freediving.

You can manage multiple participants, track time individually, run a shared session timer, and review results after the session.

Key features

  • Up to 10 participants in one session
  • Individual and global timers
  • Minute vibration notifications
  • Session history
  • Multiple languages
  • No ads, no tracking

Privacy

Group STA does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device.
